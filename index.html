Our Story

Born in Lagos.
Raised in London.

What started as a home kitchen in Peckham has grown into one of London's most celebrated Nigerian dining experiences. Chef Adaeze Obi brings three generations of family recipes to every plate — no shortcuts, no compromises.

Every dish is a love letter to Nigeria. We source our locust beans from Ibadan, our palm oil from Ondo State, and our spices from the markets where our grandmothers shopped.
